Porter

Transforming logistics with intelligent fleet management

Client Porter
Year 2023
Role Product Designer

Overview

Porter is India's largest intracity logistics platform, connecting businesses with verified truck owners. The project involved redesigning the fleet management system to improve efficiency and reduce operational costs.

Our goal was to create a seamless experience for both fleet owners and businesses while optimizing route planning and delivery tracking.

The Challenge

Porter needed to scale their operations while maintaining service quality. The key challenges included:

  • Optimize route planning to reduce fuel costs and delivery time
  • Improve fleet utilization and reduce empty trips
  • Create a unified dashboard for fleet owners and businesses
  • Implement real-time tracking and analytics

Process

Research

Studied logistics workflows, interviewed fleet owners and business users, and analyzed current pain points in the system.

Design

Created comprehensive wireframes and prototypes for the fleet management dashboard and mobile app interfaces.

Development

Collaborated with engineering teams to implement the new design system and ensure seamless integration.

Optimization

Continuously monitored performance metrics and user feedback to refine the experience.
