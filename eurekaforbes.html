Eureka Forbes

Modernizing India's trusted home care brand

Client Eureka Forbes
Year 2023
Role Brand Designer

Overview

Eureka Forbes is India's leading home care and water purification company with over 40 years of trust. The project involved modernizing their brand identity while maintaining the trust and reliability they're known for.

Our goal was to create a contemporary brand experience that resonates with both existing loyal customers and new generations of consumers.

The Challenge

Eureka Forbes needed to evolve their brand for the digital age while preserving their legacy. The main challenges were:

  • Modernize the visual identity without losing brand recognition
  • Create a cohesive experience across all touchpoints
  • Appeal to younger demographics while retaining existing customers
  • Develop a scalable design system for future growth

Process

Discovery

Conducted brand audits, customer research, and competitive analysis to understand the current brand perception and market position.

Strategy

Developed a comprehensive brand strategy that balanced heritage with innovation, defining the new brand personality and values.

Design

Created new visual identity, including logo, color palette, typography, and design system for consistent brand expression.

Implementation

Rolled out the new brand across all channels, from digital platforms to physical retail spaces and product packaging.

Results

Increase in brand awareness

40%

Growth in younger customers

95%

Customer retention rate
