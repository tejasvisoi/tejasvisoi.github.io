Obvious New Website

Redesigning the digital presence of a creative agency

Client Obvious
Year 2023
Role Web Designer

Overview

Obvious is a creative agency known for their innovative approach to design and branding. The project involved completely redesigning their website to better showcase their portfolio and attract new clients.

Our goal was to create a modern, engaging website that reflects their creative spirit while maintaining professionalism and usability.

The Challenge

Obvious needed a website that would stand out in the competitive creative industry. The main challenges were:

  • Create a unique visual identity that reflects their creative approach
  • Showcase their diverse portfolio effectively
  • Improve user engagement and lead generation
  • Ensure fast loading times and mobile responsiveness

Process

Discovery

Analyzed their current website, studied competitor sites, and conducted stakeholder interviews to understand their vision and goals.

Design

Created wireframes and high-fidelity designs focusing on visual impact, user experience, and brand consistency.

Development

Built the website using modern web technologies, ensuring optimal performance and cross-browser compatibility.

Launch

Deployed the website and implemented analytics to track performance and user behavior for ongoing optimization.
