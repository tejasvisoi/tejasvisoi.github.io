Ekana Club

Creating an exclusive digital experience for premium members

Client Ekana Club
Year 2023
Role UX Designer

Overview

Ekana Club is a premium lifestyle platform that offers exclusive experiences and services to its members. The project involved designing a sophisticated mobile app that reflects the luxury and exclusivity of the brand.

Our goal was to create an intuitive, elegant interface that enhances the member experience while maintaining the premium feel of the brand.

The Challenge

Ekana Club needed a digital platform that would match their high-end brand positioning. The main challenges were:

  • Create a luxury feel while maintaining usability
  • Design for high-net-worth individuals with specific needs
  • Integrate multiple services seamlessly
  • Ensure privacy and security for premium users

Process

Research

Conducted interviews with existing members, studied luxury app patterns, and analyzed competitor platforms to understand user expectations.

Design

Created elegant wireframes and prototypes focusing on premium aesthetics, smooth interactions, and intuitive navigation.

Testing

Conducted usability testing with target users to validate design decisions and ensure the experience meets luxury standards.

Refinement

Iterated on feedback to perfect the user experience and ensure every detail reflects the premium brand positioning.
