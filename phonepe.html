PhonePe

Redefining digital payments with intuitive design

Client PhonePe
Year 2023
Role Senior Designer

Overview

PhonePe is one of India's leading digital payment platforms, processing millions of transactions daily. The project involved redesigning the core payment experience to make it faster, more secure, and more accessible to users across all demographics.

Our goal was to create a seamless payment experience that would encourage adoption among both urban and rural users while maintaining the highest security standards.

The Challenge

PhonePe needed to scale their platform while maintaining simplicity and security. The main challenges were:

  • Simplify the payment process for first-time users
  • Ensure security without compromising usability
  • Create a scalable design system for rapid feature development
  • Optimize for users with varying levels of digital literacy

Process

Research

Conducted extensive user research across different demographics, analyzed payment patterns, and studied security best practices.

Design

Created wireframes and prototypes focusing on simplicity, security, and accessibility for all user types.

Testing

Conducted usability testing with diverse user groups to validate design decisions and ensure inclusivity.

Launch

Rolled out the new design gradually, monitoring key metrics and gathering user feedback for continuous improvement.

Results
