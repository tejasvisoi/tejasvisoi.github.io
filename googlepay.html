Google Pay

Redesigning India's most trusted digital payment platform

Client Google
Year 2023
Role Product Designer

Overview

Google Pay is India's leading digital payment platform, used by millions of users daily. The project involved redesigning the core user experience to make digital payments more intuitive, secure, and accessible to users across all demographics.

Our goal was to simplify the payment flow while maintaining the trust and reliability that users expect from Google Pay.

The Challenge

Google Pay needed to evolve beyond just a payment app to become a comprehensive financial services platform. The challenge was to:

  • Simplify complex payment flows for first-time users
  • Maintain security while improving usability
  • Create a scalable design system for future features
  • Ensure accessibility across different devices and user types

Process

Research

Conducted user interviews, analyzed usage patterns, and studied competitor apps to understand user needs and pain points.

Design

Created wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs focusing on simplicity and user trust.

Testing

Conducted usability testing with diverse user groups to validate design decisions and iterate on feedback.

Launch

Collaborated with engineering teams to ensure pixel-perfect implementation and smooth rollout.
